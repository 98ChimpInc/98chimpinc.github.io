# Loomi ‚Äî Product Brief for 98chimp.com

> **Purpose:** Give the developer building 98chimp.com everything they need to add Loomi as a product on the site ‚Äî positioning, narrative, copy direction, and how it connects to the existing three products.

---

## Why Loomi Belongs on 98chimp.com

The three existing products (DK Derby, BrainFit, Unison) help adults see themselves more clearly. Loomi works at the other end of the timeline ‚Äî before the gaps form. It helps children build confidence, self-worth, and emotional resilience during the window when the brain is most open to it: right before sleep.

Together, the four products tell a complete story: 98%Chimp builds neuroscience-grounded tools for every stage of self-awareness ‚Äî from the foundations laid in early childhood, to the recalibrations needed in adulthood.

---

## What Loomi Is

An audio-first bedtime story app for children ages 0‚Äì6. Parents start a story, place the phone face-down, and the child drifts to sleep. Professionally narrated stories have positive affirmations woven naturally into the dialogue ‚Äî not as lists, but dissolved into the narrative so children absorb confidence-building messages without knowing they're being taught anything.

Stories play over a soft lullaby melody. When the narration ends, the melody continues until the sleep timer runs out. The whole experience is designed to be started and forgotten ‚Äî anti-screen-time by design.

**Status:** Closed TestFlight Beta (Feb 2026)
**Own brand:** [loomi.kids](https://www.loomi.kids)
**Team:** Built by all three Zangenehpour brothers ‚Äî Shahin (neuroscience), Soushiant (social science), Shawn (business strategy) ‚Äî all dads.

## The Loomi Formula

Three pillars, each backed by peer-reviewed research:

1. **Storytelling** ‚Äî Builds language, imagination, and bonding rituals
2. **Affirmations** ‚Äî Shapes identity through repetition during formative years; grows confidence and eases anxiety
3. **Deep Sleep** ‚Äî Supports memory consolidation, growth, immunity, and emotional resilience

The landing page cites 8 studies from: *Sleep*, *Science*, *PLoS ONE*, *Social Cognitive & Affective Neuroscience*, *Annual Review of Psychology*, *Healthcare*, and *Scientific Reports*.

## What Makes Loomi Different from the Other Three

The developer should understand these distinctions when integrating:

- **Audience:** The other three products are for adults. Loomi is for parents of young children (0‚Äì6).
- **Team:** DK Derby, BrainFit, and Unison are Shahin's solo projects. Loomi is a collaboration with his brothers Soushiant and Shawn ‚Äî three dads building for their own daughters.
- **Philosophy:** The adult tools help you *see yourself clearly*. Loomi helps children *build the foundation* before self-awareness gaps form.
- **Own brand:** Loomi has its own landing page (loomi.kids), its own visual identity, and its own GitHub org (TricycleLabz). The 98chimp card should link out to loomi.kids rather than trying to contain all the Loomi content.
